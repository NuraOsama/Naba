About NABA

who are we?

Naba for Educational Consultancy was established by Mr. Naif AlBadrani (General Manager) on June 7, 1999, in Qassim (Buraidah), Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

We at Naba'a work specifically to help students of all nationalities who wish to travel abroad to complete postgraduate studies in different countries of the world and help them obtain a letter of acceptance from different universities with limited or complete management of their main needs.

Away from this spring we work in many fields such as: tourist visas, student visas, medical visas and document translation. We do not have other branches where we work in Qassim only and we communicate with students from different regions such as Makkah, Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, Tabuk, Hail, Abha, Khamis Mushait, Taif and many other places.

We are in the spring offering the best places to study for our students in distinguished organizations and high level of education. The educational agency is registered worldwide in all international educational organizations.

Our vision

The group's goal is to provide distinctive educational services not only in the Qassim region (Buraidah) but throughout the Kingdom.
